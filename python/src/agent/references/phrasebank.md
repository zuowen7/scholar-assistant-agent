# 学术短语库

使用本文件在明确段落修辞任务后获取短语级别的支持。

## 证据强度

### Strong（仅在实验设计+数据充分时使用）
show, demonstrate, establish, reveal, identify

### Moderate（解释合理但非定论时使用）
suggest, indicate, support the view that, are consistent with, point to

### Speculative（超出直接观察时使用）
may reflect, could arise from, appears to, seems likely, might be explained by

## 过渡词族

### 对比
however, by contrast, nevertheless, despite this, whereas

### 递进
furthermore, in addition, moreover, also

### 因果
therefore, thus, consequently, as a result, thereby

### 限定
notably, importantly, approximately, in part, at least in this cohort

## 缺口表述（精确胜于夸张）

- remains poorly understood
- has not been examined in ...
- has received limited attention
- few studies have addressed ...
- evidence remains sparse for ...

避免：no one has ever studied, completely unknown, ignored by all previous work

## 局限性表述模式

- These findings should be interpreted with caution because ...
- A limitation of this study is that ...
- The generalisability of these results is limited by ...
- We cannot exclude the possibility that ...
- Another source of uncertainty is ...

## 含义表述模式

- An implication of this is that ...
- These findings may help to explain ...
- These data support further investigation of ...
- This work has implications for ...
