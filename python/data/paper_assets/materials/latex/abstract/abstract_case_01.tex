\begin{abstract}
Academic writing assistants often improve local sentence quality but still struggle to preserve coherence across paragraphs and sections. To address this limitation, we propose a context-aware paragraph rewriting approach that incorporates surrounding discourse as additional input during revision. The proposed method is designed to improve the consistency of generated text with its neighboring content while maintaining formal academic style. This sample abstract is intended for template testing, prompt evaluation, and content-insertion experiments in paper-generation workflows.
\end{abstract}
