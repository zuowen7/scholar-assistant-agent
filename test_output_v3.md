E pigenetic alterations to chromatin (the architecture of DNA wrapped around histone proteins) affect gene expression without modifying the DNA sequence. Cowley et al . applied imiquimod to the skin of mice to induce psoriasis-like inflammation, which self-resolves within 30 days.
